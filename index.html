The Ugly Duckling

Is a fairy tale by Hans Christian Andersen is about a cygnet who looks different than the rest of it’s duck family. He endures a great deal of abuse until he matures into a beautiful swan.

The Swan Lake

is a famous ballet based on "The White Duck" and "The Stolen Veil" fairytales by Johann Karl August Musäus, The ballet was compsed by Pyotr Ilyich Tchaikovsky in 1875 and is loved by many ballet enthusiast. It’s a tale about Princess Odette and her companions who are turned into swans by an evil owl-like sorcerer Rothbart. In order for the spell to be broken, a man must fall in love with her and she with him.

The Wild Swans

Is a fairy tale by Hans Christian Andersen about a princess Elisa and her eleven brothers. The brothers are under a spell of an evil queen which causes them to turn into swans by day and humans by night. To save her brothers, Elisa has to knit 11 jackets out of stinging nettles. Once the brothers dress in nettles jackets, their bodies are turned to human form and the evil spell is broken.

The Holocaust Memorial Park

Overlooking the Sheepshead Bay is a Holocaust Memorial to millions of murdered Jews in Europe during World War II. The memorial underscores the preservation of life. How ironic, it is now also a place where the swans are being completely exterminated, although, this extermination will be achieved in a humane way.
