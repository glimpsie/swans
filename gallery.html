The Holocaust Memorial Park

The Holocaust Memorial Park overlooking the Sheepshead Bay is a memorial to millions of murdered Jews in Europe during World War II. The memorial underscores the preservation of life. How ironic, it is now also a place where the swans are being completely exterminated, although, this extermination will be achieved in a humane way.
